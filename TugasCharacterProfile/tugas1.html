Basic Attack Skill Ultimate Talent
Deals Quantum DMG equal to 15%—33% of Tribbie's Max HP to one designated enemy. Deals Quantum DMG equal to 7.5%—16.5% of Tribbie's Max HP to adjacent targets. Gains "Numinosity," lasting for 3 turn(s). This duration decreases by 1 at the start of this unit's every turn. While Tribbie has "Numinosity," increases all ally targets' All-Type RES PEN by 12%—26.4%. Activates a Zone and deals Quantum DMG equal to 15%—33% of Tribbie's Max HP to all enemies. While the Zone lasts, increases enemy targets' DMG taken by 15%—33%. After an ally target attacks, for every 1 target hit, deals 1 instance of Quantum Additional DMG equal to 6%—13.2% of Tribbie's Max HP to the target that has the highest HP among the hit targets. The Zone lasts for 2 turn(s). This duration decreases by 1 at the start of this unit's every turn. After other ally characters use Ultimate, Tribbie launches Follow-up ATK, dealing Quantum DMG equal to 9%—19.8% of Tribbie's Max HP to all enemies. This effect triggers up to 1 time per character. When Tribbie uses Ultimate, resets the trigger count for other ally characters. If the target was defeated before the Follow-up ATK is launched, then launches the Follow-up ATK against new enemy targets entering the battlefield.
